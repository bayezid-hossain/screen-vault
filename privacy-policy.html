Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

1. Information Collection

ScreenVault is a local-first application. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data, screenshots, or any other information to external servers. All processing and organization happen entirely on your device.

2. Permissions

The app requires the following permissions to function:

  • Media Library Access: To detect, organize, and manage screenshots on your device.
  • Notifications: To provide optional organization reminders.

3. Data Deletion

If you delete a screenshot within the app, it is moved to a temporary local trash state and can be restored. If you permanently delete it, the reference is removed from our local database. We do not delete the original files from your device unless explicitly requested.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising services.

5. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
